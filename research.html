Field of study

My research focuses on the remote sensing of volcanic particles from ground- and satellite-based systems using physical-electromagnetic models and statistical learning theory. I mainly develop advanced algorithms for the microphysical modelling of volcanic particles, observed by satellite radiometers in the Thermal-Infrared (TIR) and Micro- Millimetre-Wave (MW-MMW) spectrum region.

Pop me a message if you want to discuss about science and join me on a journey to discover volcanoes!
